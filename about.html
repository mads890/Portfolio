All About Me!

I have always loved doing puzzles, and coding is the most challenging puzzle I have encountered. The prospective opportunities and technologies made possible by coding are exciting, the work is engaging, and finishing a project is exceptionally rewarding. I got into this field for fun, but I have developed a passion for the process like none other that I have found in the workplace.

Art has been a part of my life since the very beginning. Art is therapeutic. It is interactive. It is stimulating. It takes practice and discipline. Art helps me see the world differently.

Physical fitness is important to me! Keeping a healthy body allows for a healthy mind. Dance--another form of art in my opinion--is a form of self-expression like no other. Dance helps me relieve stress and literally keeps me on my toes so that I am at my best every day.
